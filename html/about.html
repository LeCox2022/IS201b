Hello, my name is LeAnne.

While getting a formal college degree is an important step in many people's lives, I decided to start a family with my husband before my degree was finished.

Being a stay-at-home mom- of three, now fully grown children, and a wife and homemaker on a tight budget has given me an education that no college could come close to.

Over the years I have read books, gone to workshops & classes, researched on the internet and learned more skills through hands-on, trial-and-error and lots of practice. I have helped my husband run several small businesses- in three different states and had 'in-the-moment' and 'on-the-job' training. Mistakes, lots of practice and repeats are wonderful teachers.

I am currently working on the Bachelor's degree which was started previous to beginning my family. I am working on a Bachelor of General Studies with an emphasis on Business Management at Brigham Young University's BGS program, online.
