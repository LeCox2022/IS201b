Coming Soon Upcoming Projects

In this section I plan to share upcoming projects and possible items for my shop. If you have visited my Etsy shop, you will notice that I started down the soap making path last fall. I have loved every moment of this process. My favorite part (ok, so all of the steps are my favorite...except clean up- but it's all part of the job) is coming up with new combinations of herbs and essential oils for just the right scent and qualities, along with the ideal soap base.

I have taken a break from my current soap making schedule to finish up my current online college class and do a little organizing around the house. Too much stuff and too little space! Topic for a future blog..?!

So what is next for my shop?

    I have new plants growing that I will be drying.
    Old glass bottles which we have found on our property out in the country (we think it was a trash dump in the woods near our driveway- lots of shoe soles, various metal lids and machine parts and glass- lots and lots of glass). These are harder to clean up that you might think with all of the years of hard water staining the insides.
    Salves. I have started formulating some lovely scented skin healing salves with various qualities. My all time go-to favorite for scrapes and skin irritation features Lambs Ear and calendula herbs in an olive and coconut oil(s) base.
    Travel (or at home) shave kits with some of my handmade shave soap and a few other fun items.
    And...just about anything else I feel like making a bunch of...still just trying to find my niche.
